FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS




How do you conduct your teaching?
We primarily provide online classes, preferably using platforms such as Zoom or Google Meet.
How much time will it take to complete a course?
Your progress and the timely completion of assignments and exercises depend on your level of engagement. The more involved you are, the faster we can potentially finish the course, possibly even ahead of the designated timeline.
Is it possible to withdraw from a course midway?
We strongly advise against doing so. Nevertheless, you have the freedom to withdraw, but please note that it won't be officially recognized or endorsed.
What is the daily teaching duration?
We deliver a high-quality teaching experience. You have the flexibility to select a time that aligns with your schedule, and we assure you of our utmost dedication during that time.
When is the fee payment due?
You'll have the opportunity for two trial sessions. If you decide to proceed with us, you'll need to submit the course fee at that point.
is the timeline same for all courses?
No, the time required to complete a course varies from course to course.
Can I enroll in multiple programming courses simultaneously?
Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses at once. Our flexible learning platform allows you to tailor your learning experience to your needs.
Are your instructors experienced in the field of programming?
Absolutely! Our instructors are seasoned professionals with years of experience in the programming industry. They are dedicated to providing you with quality education.
Do I need any prior programming experience to join the courses?
Our courses are designed to accommodate learners of all levels, including beginners. No prior experience is required, but having basic computer skills can be beneficial.
What kind of support is available if I encounter difficulties while learning?
We offer comprehensive support, including Q&A sessions, instructor office hours, and a dedicated support team. We're here to help you succeed.
